I'm Kathryn Peters (you can also call me Katy).

I’m a civic technologist and non-profit entrepreneur. In 2010, I co-founded Democracy Works, and over the next ten years I led the organization’s products, programs, and operations as it grew to a team of 50 and served millions of voters.

In 2020, I joined the University of North Carolina's Center for Information, Technology, & Pubic Life (CITAP) as its inaugural executive director. At CITAP, I support a collaborative, multidisciplinary team of academics working to understand how our civic and digital lives intersect.

In my work, I'm interested in how technology serves (and fails) communities and how to design systems (technical and otherwise) that serve real human needs in all their non-binary complexities.
